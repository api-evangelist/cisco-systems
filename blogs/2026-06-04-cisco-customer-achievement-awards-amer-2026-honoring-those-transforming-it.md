Cisco has announced the winners of its 2026 Americas Customer Achievement Awards, recognizing organizations that achieved transformative IT outcomes in AI infrastructure, workplace modernization, cybersecurity, network reliability, and sustainability. Award recipients ranged from McCain Foods and Amazon in AI categories to United Airlines and TIAA in digital resilience, alongside individual recognition for customer leaders and Cisco advocates.
