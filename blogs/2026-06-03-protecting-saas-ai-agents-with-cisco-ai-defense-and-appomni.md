Enterprise AI agents deployed in SaaS platforms like ServiceNow and Microsoft 365 face growing security threats that traditional security tools struggle to address. Cisco AI Defense and AppOmni have integrated to extend runtime guardrails to SaaS-based AI agents, enabling organizations to apply uniform, customizable protections against threats like prompt injection and data exfiltration across all their agents regardless of deployment location.
