Enterprise networks are undergoing fundamental transformation as autonomous AI agents replace human users as primary network consumers, requiring new silicon capabilities. Cisco Silicon One has been purpose-built to address this shift, delivering the performance, security, and adaptability required for agentic AI operations at scale, alongside the newly announced Cisco Cloud Control unified platform integrating hardware, software, and silicon.
